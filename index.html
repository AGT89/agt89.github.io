Welcome

I'm an experienced senior software developer who holds a First-class degree in Computer Science with Business Management from the University of Birmingham. I'm currently working as a Senior Software Developer for Micro Focus in Cambridge, UK.

I have a wealth of experience in the software development industry from small scale websites to software running in the world's largest private hosted cloud and numerous things in between. These days I predominantly work in Java, but I'm not tied to a specific language and am always willing to use something different if it's the right tool for the job.

I am adept at working on a wide range of software projects including modern microservice based architectures as well as legacy projects with huge codebases and many of the issues that commonly come with such projects. With any project I'm involved in, I appreciate and contribute to good design and architecture leading to clean, re-usable and testable code.

I'm experienced in agile methodologies and have been Scrum Master for a number of teams in my development career. I'm experienced in working with all levels of an organisation, from discussions and planning with project managers and non-technical stakeholders through to training junior members of the team.

My current role at Micro Focus sees me taking part in developing and architecting parts of the Digital Safe system that helps some of the largest financial institutions in the world meet their compliance and regulatory requirements.
